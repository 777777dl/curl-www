Of course there is constant development going on in curl land. In order to help out the development team, we have a set of different services setup.

Recent CVS Activity - You wanna know what happened recently in the curl CVS repository?

Automatic Builds - Friendly people help us build curl automaticly in an automated manner so that we can verify that it remains buildable and working on a larger variety of platform, even when bigger changes are made.

Daily Snapshot - The latest CVS version of curl is automaticly packaged and uploaded every single day.

libcurl mailing list - Most of the development talk and thoughts about how the future will become is discussed there. In fact, you can't be seriously interested in curl development without being subscribed to at least that one.

Bug tracker - people submit their found problems to the bug tracker as it makes it easy for the development crew to keep track of all remaining problems. See also the KNOWN_BUGS document.

Feedback forum - similar to the bug tracker, but not really concerning bugs, the feedback forum is a place to stop by and read what users think of curl and the curl future.
