The main curl mailing list is about everything that concerns curl. Questions, bug reports, scripts and related tools and subjects are welcome there! We also tend to discuss bugs, future developments and what curl should not do.

To Join:
Mail curl-request at contactor.se with a body that contains subscribe your@email.adress.

To Post:
Write to the list by mailing to curl at contactor.se.

To Leave:
Mail curl-request at contactor.se with a body that contains unsubscribe your@email.adress.

Replace 'at' with '@' to create valid email-adresses!
