Curl, like other well-designed Unix software, does not suffer from a Year 2000 issue. Unix dates are based around a 32-bit field centered around January 1 00:00:00 1970, with a range from Dec 13, 1901 to January 19, 2038. We therefore anticipate no impact on any of our Unix or win32 implementations because of this new millenium.
